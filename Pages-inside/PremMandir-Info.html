Description

Prem Mandir, located in the holy town of Vrindavan, Uttar Pradesh, is a monumental temple dedicated to the theme of divine love. "Prem" translates to love, and the temple is a celebration of the eternal love between Radha and Krishna.


Enchanting Stories

  • The temple is adorned with intricate carvings that narrate the stories of Radha and Krishna. Every inch of the temple reflects the craftsmanship and attention to detail, creating a mesmerizing tapestry of art and spirituality.
  • Prem Mandir is renowned for its captivating light and sound show. In the evening, the temple is illuminated, and the surroundings come alive with a spectacular presentation that narrates the tales of love from Hindu scriptures. The temple is a significant hub for the Bhakti movement, emphasizing the path of devotion and love toward the divine.
