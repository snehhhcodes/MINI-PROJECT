Description

ISKCON, commonly known as the Hare Krishna movement, is a global spiritual phenomenon founded by A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada in 1966. With its roots in ancient Indian philosophy, ISKCON has grown into a worldwide spiritual and cultural movement.


Enchanting Stories

  • Central to the practice of ISKCON is the chanting of the Maha Mantra: "Hare Krishna, Hare Krishna, Krishna Krishna, Hare Hare, Hare Rama, Hare Rama, Rama Rama, Hare Hare." This mantra is believed to have transformative and purifying effects on the mind and soul.
  • Iskcon has established breathtaking temples around the globe, each a masterpiece of architecture and spiritual ambiance. It is also known for its commitment to providing sanctified vegetarian food, known as prasadam, to its visitors and devotees.
