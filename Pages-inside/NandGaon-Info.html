Description

Nandgaon is believed to be the village where Lord Krishna spent his childhood with his foster parents, Nanda and Yashoda. Nand Bhavan in Nandgaon is considered the house of Nanda Maharaj, Lord Krishna's foster father. Pilgrims visit this site to witness the place where Krishna is said to have grown up.


Enchanting Stories

  • Nandgaon is associated with Radha Kunda and Shyama Kunda, two sacred ponds linked to the divine love of Radha and Krishna. Devotees believe that Radha Kunda was created by Radha and Shyama Kunda by Lord Krishna himself.
  • Nandgaon is renowned for its unique Lathmar Holi tradition, where women playfully "beat" men with sticks, reenacting the divine love play between Radha and Krishna.
