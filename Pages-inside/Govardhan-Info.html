Govardhan Blends Natural Beauty With Sacred Significance

  1. Home
  2. Govardhan

Description

Govardhan Hill, situated in the sacred town of Vrindavan, is more than just a geological formation; it's a symbol of devotion and divine miracles. Towering over the landscape, this majestic hill has been witness to the enchanting leelas (pastimes) of Lord Krishna.


Enchanting Stories

  • One of the most famous stories associated with Govardhan is the miraculous event of Lord Krishna lifting the hill with his little finger. According to mythology, Krishna held the hill aloft for seven days to protect the residents of Vrindavan from the wrath of Lord Indra's storms.
  • Performing the Govardhan Parikrama, a circumambulation of the hill, is considered a sacred tradition. Pilgrims embark on this spiritual journey, walking the path that Lord Krishna is believed to have taken during his divine pastimes.
